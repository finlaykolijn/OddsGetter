Premier League Fixtures 2024/2025

The Premier League 2024/2025 season features 38 matches per team, with home and away fixtures, just like every other season. However, the teams involved change each year due to relegation and promotion. This season, Luton Town, Burnley, and Sheffield United were relegated to the Championship, while Leicester City, Ipswich Town, and Southampton have been promoted to the EPL.

As the season progresses, especially during the busy Christmas period, fixtures often include midweek games or matches at unusual times. To stay informed and up-to-date with PL odds, be sure to check OddsPortal regularly for updated schedules. Alongside Premier League action, clubs also compete in other tournaments like the FA Cup and EFL Cup throughout the season.

Additionally, OddsPortal offers odds comparisons, in-play odds, standings, and match facts for top European competitions like the Champions League, Europa League, and Europa Conference League, in which certain Premier League teams also participate.

Premier League Standings and Form

Our live Premier League standings not only inform you of the position of each team, but a lot more than that too. Firstly, the amount of games played, won, lost, drawn, and points accumulated will be visible as expected.

But additionally, our EPL standings will tell you the amount of goals scored and goals conceded, concluding in each team’s goal difference, and finally the form for each team in their most recent five matches in the Premier League.

Moreover, you can check the form for 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, and 30 previous matches by firstly clicking on “form” below all the seasons available, where you can click to select the amount of games you want to see of a team’s form.

Finally, you are also able to see the form both home and away, to enable you to judge whether a team performs well away from home, poorly at home, or another trend as the season progresses.
